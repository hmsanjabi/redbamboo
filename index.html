Our Story

London Heritage.
Oriental Authenticity.

Established for almost a decade in North London, Red Bamboo is a family-owned establishment born from a desire to share the genuine warmth of a traditional Chinese home. Our restaurant serves as a landmark of culinary consistency, where the history of our family meets the vibrant energy of the city.

We take pride in our identity as a quintessential Oriental kitchen, remaining a steadfast sanctuary for those seeking honest, time-honored flavors in a welcoming environment.

Food & Craft

The Wok &
The Hearth.

Our menu is a living archive of traditional Chinese techniques. Every preparation is grounded in the integrity of the material and the mastery of the flame, delivering the ephemeral smoky complexity that defines Oriental culinary excellence.

Warm Welcome

Experience the genuine hospitality of a family restaurant where every guest is treated as an extension of our own home.

Takeaway Service
